My Research.

Self & Emotion

I am investigating how human processes our own facial expressions differently from others' facial expressions with electrophysiological measure.

Music & Emotion

I am investigating human's neural mechanism of music-evoked emotions with electrophysiological measure.

Evolution of Music

I am investigating in which species music arose by focusing on the music-evoked emotions between primates and human beings.

Visual Motion Perception

I investigated how language influences human's streaming and bouncing percepts. It turns out that when the word labels representing "streaming" and "bouncing" were removed, the tendency of perceiving bouncing percept was also disappeared.
