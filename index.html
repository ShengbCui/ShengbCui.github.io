Current Expeditions

  • Interoceptionx Investigate how heartbeat-evoked potentials (HEP) and related bodily signals are represented in cortex and how these interoceptive cues shape perception, attention, and valuation across tasks.
  • Social Decision-Making Study neural mechanisms of moral choices and face-based trustworthiness judgments, testing how interoceptive processing (e.g., HEP) and large-scale networks such as precuneus/PCC and vmPFC contribute to evaluating others and guiding social behavior.
  • Computational Modeling I use inverted encoding models to reconstruct orientation-selective population codes, examining how task demands and attention modulate the fidelity of visual feature representations.
